about gwp jamshedpur

Government Women's Polytechnic Jamshedpur is came into existence in the year 1998. Situtated in Gamharia, Sareikela-Kharsawn district of Jharkhand near Jamshedpur. The Institution is offering Diploma Programmes in Computer Science Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering & Architecture Engineering. Presently the Institution building is in the 9 acres of land where complete building has developed desired quality and standard institution also has qualified faculty and other resources to suit with fast changing technological innovation and inventions.GWP Jamshedpur takes extreme pride in the fact that it has been occupying a unique position in imparting technological education to Indian Women youth. Since its inception, the institute has had a vision to provide quality technical education and to facilitate scientific and technological research, coupled with a mission to develop human potential to its zenith.

CURRICULUM

The college is affiliated to AICTE and JUT, Ranchi (Jharkhand University of Technology, Ranchi), the curriculam is according to JUT, Ranchi who is also responsible for conducting exams and awarding degrees and certifications to students who successfully pass the exam. There are 2 types of examinations conducted 2 MID SEMESTER(SNAP TEST) and 1 END SEMESTER in every semester of 6 months period. SNAP TEST are conducted as internal examination of college based on which students evaluation is done along with projects, assingments, viva-voce etc. External Examinations are conducted by board JUT, Ranchi. There are total 6 semesters (3 years) for entry level and 4 semesters (2 years) for lateral entry students.

GWP Jamshedpur offers diploma in 4 branches of engineering :

  1. Mechanical Engineering
  2. Architecture Assistantship
  3. Electronics and Communication Engineering
  4. Computer Science Engineering
